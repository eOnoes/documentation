### Viewer Preferences by Age
- **18-24**: Energetic, conversational. Less tolerant of robotic sound. Higher AI acceptance. American accents preferred.
- **25-34**: Largest YouTube demo. Tolerate AI if quality is high. Warm, authoritative tones preferred.
- **35-50**: More discerning, detect AI more readily. British accents preferred for educational content.
- **50+**: Least accepting. Higher bounce rates when AI detected. Prefer human narration.

### Key Insight
Voice quality/prosody matters MORE than whether the voice is "real." Viewers care about how it sounds, not whether it's human. Voice cloning using own voice is the fastest-growing trend.

## PART 2: AI Character Visual Style Preferences

### Engagement Rankings by Style
| Style | Engagement | Best For |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Stylized/Cartoon** | HIGHEST | Educational, explainer, short-form |
| **Anime-style** | HIGH | Entertainment, storytelling, gaming |
| **Cel-shaded/Comic** | MODERATE-HIGH | Action, superhero, branded content |
| **Hyper-realistic CGI** | MODERATE | Film/marketing, corporate |
| **Photorealistic AI avatars** | POLARIZING | News, corporate |

### The Uncanny Valley — Still Real
- Audiences identify AI-generated humans with ~70-80% accuracy
- Mouth movement and eye tracking are biggest triggers
- Once told a character is AI, discomfort drops ~40% (disclosure helps)
- Western audiences show strong uncanny valley for photorealism
- **This pushes creators toward stylized aesthetics**

### Best Styles for Educational Content
1. **Stylized 2D/Cartoon** — highest info retention, lowest cognitive load
2. **Whiteboard/hand-drawn** — strong for step-by-step processes
3. **3D stylized (Pixar-adjacent)** — excellent for younger audiences
4. **Stylized avatar** — growing category for corporate training
5. **Hyper-realistic** — LESS effective for education, perceived as less trustworthy

### Age Demographics & Style
| Age Group | Preferred Style | Avoid |
|-----------|----------------|-------|
| Gen Alpha (10-15) | Cartoon, bright colors | Hyper-realistic ("weird") |
| Gen Z (16-28) | Anime, stylized 2D | Photorealistic (skepticism) |
| Millennials (29-44) | Varied (context-dependent) | Overly "kiddie" cartoon |
| Gen X (45-60) | Realistic/professional | Excessive stylization |
| Boomers (61+) | Realistic, familiar broadcast | Obviously "AI" |

### Eddie's Vision = PERFECT FIT
**Hyper-realistic comic book / cel-shaded style** = MODERATE-HIGH engagement
- Avoids uncanny valley completely
- Appeals strongly to Gen Z and Millennials
- Works for educational content
- Signals "this is AI" which INCREASES trust with younger audiences
- The "honest about being AI" factor is key for Gen Z

## RECOMMENDATIONS FOR EDDIE'S CHANNEL

### Character Design
- **Male character**: Young, approachable, relatable. Cel-shaded/hyper-realistic comic style.
- **Female AI assistant**: Same visual style. Personality-driven. The "you are hopeless" dynamic.
- **Style**: Cel-shaded / hyper-realistic comic book = BEST FIT
  - Avoids uncanny valley
  - Appeals to target demo (18-34)
  - Signals "this is AI" which builds trust
  - Works for educational content
  - Allows for expressive characters without realism constraints

### Voice Selection
- **Male**: Warm, conversational, slightly confused/curious (relatable beginner energy)
- **Female**: Clear, confident, slightly snarky (the expert who enjoys teaching)
- Both should sound natural and conversational, not "announcer-like"
- Consider using MiMo VoiceDesign to create custom voices (like we did for Cyony)
- Voice cloning of Eddie's own voice for the male character = authenticity + no AI stigma

### Content Strategy
- Educational/explainer format = highest performing niche
- Dynamic between characters = what hooks viewers
- The recurring gags (typo zoom, "you are hopeless", mute snark) = what keeps them coming back
- Consistent upload schedule = AI's biggest advantage over human creators

### Target Demographic
- **Primary**: Gen Z (16-28) — prefer stylized, high AI acceptance
- **Secondary**: Millennials (29-44) — versatile, appreciate quality
- **Avoid targeting**: 50+ — least accepting of AI content
